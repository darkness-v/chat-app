### 1. Streaming Architecture

  1: CodeExecutor(dataframes={"df": DataFrame1}),

matplotlib â†’ BytesIO â†’ base64 â†’ JSON â†’ Frontend â†’ <img src="data:image/png;base64,..."/>

### 5. Automatic Retry with LLM
